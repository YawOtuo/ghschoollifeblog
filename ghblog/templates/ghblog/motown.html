Life in Achimota School

She is the head of all the hosts, the school of whom we boast, she is the glory of the coast and her name is Achimota. Motown prides herself as one of the IS schools in the country, Ghana. With their black and white colors, they boast some of the best leaders and thinkers Ghana has ever produced.

How is life in the school like though? Here is the story of an unnamed person

For me Motown was fun and stressful. Motown has some of the best facilities in any school in Ghana. With fully well-equipped labs, smartly architecture on only a few of the large acres of land present in the school, the teaching and learning experience has been made very interactive. I was taught by devoted and friendly teachers.

My best experience was when during the inter school games my crush placed his hand in mine, sending chills down my spine to the earth and through to my future

Surely, the presence of mixed gender alone makes the school itself an entertaining experience itself, if you know what I mean. And oh my! The bon fire nights can be so crazy.

Achimota is one school that places huge emphasizes on decency, decorum and decency. School taught me how to eat with a fork and knife, taught me to never give up after failing several times and most of all that there are some really great friends one can make in life.
